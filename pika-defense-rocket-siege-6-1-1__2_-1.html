HOW TO PLAY

1️⃣ Pick a Pokemon from the tray below, then tap an empty tile on the path to place it.

2️⃣ Towers attack enemies in range automatically. Match the type for 2x damage (super effective) — wrong type = half damage.

3️⃣ Tap a placed tower to upgrade or sell it.

4️⃣ Don't let enemies reach the end of the path — it costs you lives!

5️⃣ Every 5 waves, a Rocket Boss appears — stronger, and needs a mixed-type strategy.
